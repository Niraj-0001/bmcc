Bright Minds Coaching Center

Founded in 2025, Bright Minds has established itself as a premier coaching center in Bishunpur Gokhul, Aurai, Muzaffarpur, known for its academic excellence and student-centric approach. We specialize in providing comprehensive education for students from Class 1 to 10, covering both CBSE and BSEB boards.

Our teaching methodology combines traditional values with modern techniques, ensuring students develop strong fundamentals while staying ahead of contemporary educational trends.
